Our Story

WeGo was created by two San Francisco Bay Area residents that were simply tired of having their plans rained on, sometimes literally. With foggy days ruining great views, to hot days ruining great hikes, having the ability to having these activities with the weather in mind would alleviate these woes. And so WeGo was born and expanded to serve more purpose than a simple weather check.
